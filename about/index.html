About PlayEquipHub

Your trusted partner in sports equipment since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PlayEquipHub emerged from a passion for sports and a vision to provide high-quality sports equipment to athletes across India. We believe that every athlete deserves access to premium sports gear that enhances their performance.

What started as a small venture in Mumbai has now grown into one of India's most trusted sports equipment retailers. Our journey began when our founder, Rajesh Kumar, a former national-level cricket player, noticed the lack of quality sports equipment available to aspiring athletes.

Determined to make a difference, Rajesh assembled a team of sports enthusiasts and retail experts to create a platform that would bridge this gap. Today, PlayEquipHub serves thousands of customers across the country, from professional athletes to weekend warriors.

Our Mission

To empower athletes of all levels by providing them with the best sports equipment, expert guidance, and a seamless shopping experience.

We are committed to supporting the athletic journey of every individual, from beginners taking their first steps to professionals competing at the highest levels.
